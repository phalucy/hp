Presentation at the Seminar for Extreme Weathers @Nanjing University

I’m pleased to give a presentation at Nanjing University and shared my works in tracing moisture sources of heavy rainfalls. During the seminar, Prof. Lei Zhao (University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign) also presented his wonderful works and shared his ideas in atmospheric boundary layer and urban microweather systems.
