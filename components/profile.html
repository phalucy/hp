About me

I'm interested in the short-term air-sea interactions and extreme events, including the processes between extra-tropical cyclones and marginal seas, interactions between the upper ocean and the diurnal cycle within the Maritime Continent, heavy rainfalls and atmospheric rivers (AR), marine heatwaves, and their impacts on ecosystems.

Keywords: Air-Sea interaction; Extreme events; Moisture transport; Extratropical cyclones and marginal seas
